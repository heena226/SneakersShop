Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting our sneakers e-commerce website. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers' personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

Information Collection

When you use our website, we may collect the following types of personal information from you:

  • Contact information (name, email address, phone number, shipping and billing address)
  • Payment information (credit or debit card number)
  • Order history and preferences
  • Device and browser information (IP address, browser type, device type, and operating system)
  • Website usage information (pages visited, time spent on website)

Information Use

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To communicate with you about your orders and provide customer support
  • To personalize your shopping experience and improve our website
  • To prevent fraud and protect our business
  • Website usage information (pages visited, time spent on website)

Third-Party Access

We may share your personal information with third-party companies that provide services to our website, such as payment processing, shipping, and analytics. We will only share the information necessary for these companies to provide their services, and we will ensure that they have appropriate privacy and security measures in place.

Security Measures

We take the security of your personal information seriously and use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. This includes encrypting sensitive data and regularly monitoring our systems for security threats.

Review and Update

You have the right to review and update your personal information at any time by logging into your account on our website. If you need assistance updating your information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us using the information below.

Complaints or Concerns

If you have any complaints or concerns about our privacy policy or the handling of your personal information, please contact us using the information below. We will investigate and respond to any complaints or concerns in a timely manner.
